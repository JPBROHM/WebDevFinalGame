Instructions:

  1. If the gamescreen is too big on your monitor, zoom out (Ctrl+'-') until you can see the entire gamescreen.
  2. This is a two-player competitive game, so ensure you have a friend to play with
  3. Use WASD to control the mouse, and arrow keys to control the cat
  4. Do not hold the directional keys to move, they must instead be single presses to move
  5. The mouse must collect at least 5 pieces of cheese and then return to his hide-away hole
  6. The cat must catch the mouse before he can escape
  7. If the mouse decides to go above and beyond and collect ALL 8 pieces of cheese, they will be rewarded with 2 wins
